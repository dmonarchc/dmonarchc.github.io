date: 2025-04-17
---

What is not measured cannot be improved.

I’ve come across this idea before, but I never really applied it to my own work.

From this point forward, I will document any technical work I do, whether it is coding, studying mathematics, or exploring new concepts.

The goal is not to produce content, but to track progress and make understanding explicit.

This space will serve as a record of that process.

Only what is actually done and understood will be written here.
